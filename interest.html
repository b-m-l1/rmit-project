Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline your IT experience (if any).

My interest in IT started in high school - Primarily through some work experience with a local manufacturing company. I didn’t really follow it up originally, as I found my first full time job and focused on that.
Thus, my experience in the area is a little bit sparse - I have some knowledge of taking things apart and putting them together, and some very basic knowledge of what parts are within a physical desktop.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I enquired with Open Universities Australia about IT education. Fortunately for me, it was at the right time to sign up for a course plan. I’d love to say it was well planned and plotted out, however the truth is it was really just good timing to make an enquiry to OUA.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

I expect to learn a whole lot about IT that I had little to no knowledge of before. Even during the introduction sections of this course I’ve come to realize that there’s a lot more involvement of IT in both future and current technology, and touching on some of how it’s developing.
